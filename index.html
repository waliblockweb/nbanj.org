Narsingdi-Bhairab Association of New Jersey (NBANJ)

About NBANJ

The Narsingdi-Bhairab Association of New Jersey (NBANJ) is a vibrant cultural and social organization dedicated to fostering and strengthening the cultural and social bonds of the District of Narsingdi & Bhairab, Bangladesh. NBANJ is founded with the aim of preserving and promoting the rich heritage of Narsingdi & Bhairab, the association serves as a platform for the community to come together and celebrate their shared traditions, customs, and values. Through various cultural events, festivals, and educational programs, the association endeavors to create a sense of belonging and pride among its members, while also facilitating cultural exchange and understanding with the larger community of New Jersey. With its passionate members, committee and dedicated volunteers, the Narsingdi-Bhairab Association of New Jersey plays a pivotal role in preserving and nurturing the vibrant cultural tapestry of Narsingdi & Bhairab, ensuring that its legacy continues to thrive for generations to come.
